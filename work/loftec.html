Loftec

Promo Website

Lofts and technology — the meaning behind Loftec brand name — is modern residential real estate project in Moscow. Marketed toward a younger clientele, the project provides modern, technologically advanced living in the city's centre.

A unique and inspiring website we came up with for the project conveyed a sense of high technological advancement, style, and cosines, creating an impression of the best possible choice for a young man or woman to realise their ambitions in the busy city.
