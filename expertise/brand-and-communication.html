Brand & Communication Design

Overview

A great brand identity serves as a key to your business strategy by communicating your functional message, personality, values, and aspirations. When done well, your prospective clients will intuitively and effortlessly realise how you differ from the competition, what product quality and level of service they can expect from you, just from a brief interaction with your brand. We will help you design a complete brand identity system for your product or company, from an original name, eye-catching and intelligent logo design, to a comprehensive brand book and promo material design. We will make you stand out from the crowd and be easily remembered and recognised.

Services

Communication strategy

Your customers are constantly bombarded with marketing messages. In order to stand out from the noise, your signal should be clear and precise. If it is immediately obvious how your product helps your prospective clients fulfil their life on various levels, if it feels natural to their personality and fits their life context, and, most important, if it's charged with authentic desire to help — then your message stands a chance of being heard, listened to, and actioned upon.

Whether working on a branding project or developing a creative concept for a website, we base our strategic work on a thorough understanding of your product, competitors, industry, your organisation, and your customers. The insight we gain empowers us to develop original and effective ways to position your brand against the competition, establish effective communication with your customers by presenting overlapping values, and talk in a tone of voice that is only and uniquely yours.

Brand modelling

We will model your company or product brand based on deep research into the category, prosp[ects, and competition. We will design a cohesive positioning that will help you compete and win customer devotion from brand values, character, and customers to brand core.

Visual identity

Fresh and trendy or classic and reserved, we will develop a visual identity that fits your brand tone of voice and conveys the right message to potential customers. Your company or product will be easily recognised and always remembered.

Copywriting

Your best communication is essentially a story about the richer, inspiring and more fulfilling life that your prospective clients may aspire to. The more substance there is to your story, the more effective it is for convincing your clients. We will help you forge a solid foundation for your communication with sharp strategy development and create a wide array of valuable and compelling content. From researching and writing the plot to developing high-class custom illustrations and filming photos and video, we will provide your communication with all required materials in a style and tone of voice that perfectly complements your brand.

Promo websites

Vivid, immersive and impactful, a solid promo website provides a rich interactive story about the product. It always conveys every critical attribute to your customers with style and creativity, catching their attention and igniting their interest. We will equip your marketing effort with the best tools to convert clients from catchy copywriting to immersive illustrations, modern motion graphics, and animation.

Photo and video

An image is worth a thousand words — we get more than 70% of information via optical channels, and high-grade, custom-made, uniquely produced pictures and videos are essential to convey information about your product or company to your clients and partners. From a team photoset to sophisticated product videos, we will plan and fully produce cutting-edge material that would make your communication shine.

Print design

Today, smartphones and digital media are ubiquitous, and offer a convenient way to interact with information.

However, if you want to impress your customer and ensure that they remember your company or product at home or at work, it is still a great idea to present them with a printed brochure or a book. Rich in information, interesting and valuable — a tangible product will always be closer to a heart.

3D and visualisation

We produce photorealistic, visually impactful product visualisation for various industries and media. From architectural projects to scientific product and corporate presentations. W e will plan, design and produce stunning images and motion graphics that will make your product or company stand apart from the competition.
