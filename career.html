Front-End Developer

Location

Remote

Experience

3+ years

Required skills

JavaScript, ES6, HTML, SASS/SCSS, jQuery, GIT

We are looking for a team-mate to help us develop front-end for the inspiring, animated, technically and creatively cutting-edge websites we produce. We want you to share our passion for creating clean and reusable code for award-winning websites with a stack focusing on high-end animations and maintainability based on JQuery, ES6, and CSS. We have exciting tasks for various skill levels and will support your growth and development in a friendly and result-oriented environment.

Skill requirements

  • Good knowledge of JavaScript (ES6), CSS, and HTML
  • Previous experience with jQuery library. Bonus: jQuery plugin development experience
  • Experience in the development of CSS-based animations
  • Proper use of SCSS variables, imports and exports, and mixins
  • Understanding of project bundling with Gulp
  • Knowledge of Git development workflow
  • Ability to install local web server powered by Nginx/Apache, PHP, and MySQL

Prior similar work experience would be an advantage.

We will gladly help junior developer boost their skills and gather experience. At the same time, we will be happy to work with a middle-level developer. The critical requirement is that you share a passion for creative front-end and want to develop websites similar to ours. We build web and mobile apps using the SPSa framework as Vue and React, but their number is significantly lower than the promo web, so you must want to do the creative front.

Work conditions

  • Remote work
  • Full time, full involvement, 8-hour work day
  • Compensation according to your skill level
  • A great team
  • Highly rewarding projects and clients

UX Designer

Location

Remote

Experience

5+ years

Required skills

Figma, UX patterns, UX analysis, CJM design, UI design

We seek a passionate UX designer to help us design and implement inspiring and practical digital products for our clients and ourselves. We anticipate you to have a broad experience in product and user analysis to set the right project goals and design cutting-edge user experience and UI design.

Assignment

  • Existing product analysis for UX bottleneck identification. User analysis and CJM design for the new products.
  • UX improvement hypothesis design.
  • UX prototype design and testing.
  • Top-grade UI design.
  • Frontend implementation guidance.
  • After-launch product analysis and improvements.

We're looking for a middle-level designer with experience in similar work. You would work under the supervision of the UX Lead Designer on eCommerce, Self-Care, BPM, and corporate web projects for international clients.

Skill requirements

  • At least five years of experience in similar projects.
  • Ability to understand and analyse complex applications from a business process and user experience perspective.
  • Sharp UX pattern knowledge (NNG, Baymard).
  • Great UI design experience, good taste, fresh and interesting works.
  • B2 or higher English.

Work conditions

  • Remote work.
  • Full time, full involvement, 8-hour work day.
  • Compensation according to your skill level.
  • A great team.
  • Highly rewarding projects and clients.

Symfony Developer

Location

Remote

Experience

3+ years

Required skills

Symfony, MySQL/PostgreSQL, Redis, ElasticSearch, AWS, Git

The top-grade design isn't the only attribute of our work. We build world-class digital products, and a robust backend is essential. We constantly work on major corporate websites, eCommerce, and enterprise apps. We are looking for a teammate to help us develop high-quality systems. If you have an extensive experience with Symfony, that would be fantastic! If you still need to, we would love to help you get ahead with it if you have good PHP knowledge. We have exciting tasks for various skill levels and will support your growth and development in a friendly and result-oriented environment.

Skill requirements

  • Good knowledge of PHP
  • Previous experience with Symfony framework
  • Experience in object-oriented application development
  • Understanding of design principles and patterns
  • Knowledge of PostgreSQL, MySQL, and Redis databases
  • Knowledge of Git development workflow
  • Proficiency in Linux environment
  • Understanding of REST, SOAP and etc.

Prior similar work experience would be an advantage.

We will gladly help junior developer boost their skills and gather experience. At the same time, we will be happy to work with a middle-level developer. The critical requirement is that you share a passion for clean, elegant, and efficient code and a desire to contribute to similar projects.

Work conditions

  • Remote work
  • Full time, full involvement, 8-hour work day
  • Compensation according to your skill level
  • A great team
