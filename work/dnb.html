DNB

Corporate Portal

DNB is Norway's largest financial services group. We have developed numerous digital products for the company, including the group's corporate portal, a corporate portal for Latvia, and multiple other corporate and promo websites.

The most significant product, the group's corporate portal, encompassed all countries of operations and helped automate communications at a group and country-level, allowing 3500 group employees to be up to date with the latest news and access global and local documentation and information.
